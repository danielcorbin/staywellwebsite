Focused Lab Evaluation

A structured, one-time clinical insight into your health markers. Professional interpretation for those seeking clarity before committing to ongoing care.

Clarity Through Clinical Insight

The Focused Lab Evaluation is designed for individuals who are not yet ready for a full membership but are seeking a professional, evidence-informed review of their health markers. This standalone service provides a clear snapshot of your current health status through structured data and expert interpretation.

This is a one-time evaluation focused specifically on lab data. It is not a substitute for ongoing primary care, but rather a professional bridge for those seeking deeper understanding of their health.

Transparent Pricing

Evaluation Fee

The evaluation fee covers the professional time required for the **review of your intake questionnaire** and the delivery of **personalized, actionable insights** based on your lab results.

Lab Costs (Separate)

**Actual lab costs are separate** and not included in the evaluation fee. Costs vary depending on the specific tests ordered and your clinical needs.

  • Standard labs may be billable to insurance.
  • Specialty tests (DUTCH, salivary analysis, environmental mold, etc.) are typically not billable to insurance.
  • Specialty test costs range from **$10 to $500+** depending on complexity.

Supporting Your Continuity of Care

We believe that the best health outcomes come from consistent, relationship-based care. If you find the insights from your lab evaluation valuable and choose to join a Staywell Health membership within 60 days of completing your interpretation, we will waive the one-time membership registration fee as a courtesy.

This waiver is our way of supporting your transition into a continuous primary care partnership.
