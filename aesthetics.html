Why We Choose Xeomin

Understanding the differences between Xeomin and Botox can help you make an informed decision about your aesthetic treatment.

Feature Xeomin Botox
Active Ingredient Pure botulinum toxin A Botulinum toxin A + proteins
Additives No complexing proteins Contains accessory proteins
Antibody Resistance Risk Lower risk Higher risk over time
Storage Requirements Room temperature stable Requires refrigeration
Onset of Results 3-4 days 2-3 days
Duration of Results 3-4 months 3-4 months

Why Xeomin is Our Preferred Choice

Xeomin's pure formulation without complexing proteins offers several advantages for our patients:

  • Reduced Risk of Resistance: Lower likelihood of developing antibodies that could reduce effectiveness over time
  • Pure Formula: Contains only the active ingredient needed for results
  • Consistent Quality: Room temperature stability ensures product integrity
  • Natural Results: Precise, predictable outcomes for a refreshed appearance
