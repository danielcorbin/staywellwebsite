About Staywell Health

"A re-imagining of what healthcare could look like, from primary care to hormone health, weight management, and longevity medicine, to help people truly get well - and stay well."

Who We Are

Staywell Health offers integrative membership medicine in Raleigh, NC, from primary care to hormone health, weight management, and longevity medicine, designed to treat the whole person, not just symptoms. This practice was founded with a bold vision: to do medicine differently.

After years of witnessing the limitations of traditional healthcare: rushed visits, symptom-chasing, and burnout, Staywell Health emerged as a reimagining of what healthcare could look like.

Our practice offers something deeper: a space where healing means more than prescriptions and lab results. At Staywell Health, care begins by understanding the whole person, not just a diagnosis. We look at how each person eats, moves, sleeps, manages stress, connects with others, and finds meaning.

Your Healthcare Partner

At Staywell Health, you're not just another patient in a busy schedule. You're a valued partner in your own healthcare journey, working with a dedicated provider who takes the time to truly understand your unique needs and goals.

Our membership model allows for unhurried consultations, comprehensive care planning, and the development of genuine therapeutic relationships that make all the difference in your health outcomes.

Experience the difference that personalized, relationship-based healthcare can make in your life.

Meet Daniel

Daniel Corbin, DNP, APRN, FNP-C

Daniel brings a unique perspective to healthcare, spanning primary care, hormone optimization, and longevity medicine, combining traditional medical training with a deep understanding of lifestyle medicine and integrative approaches to health.

With years of experience in primary care, Daniel has witnessed firsthand the transformative power of taking time with patients, addressing root causes, and focusing on prevention rather than just treatment.

His approach is grounded in evidence-based medicine while recognizing that true healing often requires addressing the whole person - mind, body, and spirit.

When not caring for patients, Daniel enjoys spending time with his family, staying active outdoors, and continuing his education in functional and lifestyle medicine.

Our Purpose

Our goal is simple: to help people truly get well - and stay well. For some, that means reversing chronic disease. For others, it means preventing it. And for many, it means reaching preventive lifestyle-focused care that helps them flourish.

We believe in the power of lifestyle medicine to transform health outcomes. Rather than just treating disease, we focus on the root causes that drive illness and the daily practices that build resilience.

This approach recognizes that true health isn't just the absence of disease - it's the presence of vitality, purpose, and well-being.
